Be personable

Adopt a friendly, conversational tone. We're here to help our users, not talk at them.

Be impactful

Deliver real value with every word. Include relevant, insightful, and clear explanations.

Be clear

Use direct, everyday language. No embellishments or unnecessary jargon.

Be inclusive

Use respectful, people-first language. Focus on the person, not their disability or condition.

05 — Brand foundation

Mission & values

Everything we build, say, and design flows from a clear sense of purpose and a consistent set of values.

Mission

To simplify the provision of in-home support, helping small businesses grow and deliver a higher standard of care.

Proposition

Streamlining and simplifying scheduling, invoicing, and care management to empower providers in raising the quality and efficiency of their services.

Central idea

Deliver a higher standard of care.

Audience

Small to medium care providers in aged care, disability, and in-home support — primarily in Australia.

Core values

Dependability Efficiency Compassion Sincerity Loyalty Safety Respect Humility
06 — Photography

Image guidelines

Photography should reflect the warmth and professionalism of care work. Every image should feel genuine, positive, and people-centred.

✓

Positive & engaged

Show people actively engaged with their carers or loved ones in warm, uplifting scenarios.

✓

Diverse representation

Include a diverse range of ethnicities. Keep a focus on aged care and disability contexts.

✓

Natural & authentic

Avoid overly staged or stock-photo aesthetics. Candid, real-feeling moments are preferred.

✗

No third-party logos

Do not capture other software or brand logos unless they belong to a ShiftCare partner.

✗

Avoid dark or cold tones

Photography should feel warm and optimistic — avoid clinical, grey, or overly formal imagery.

✗

No stereotyping

Portray people with dignity and agency. Do not reduce anyone to their condition or disability.
